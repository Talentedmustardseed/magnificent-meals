Which types of food do I need to eat the most?

The amount and types of food depends on your age, gender, weight and other variables. But for most adolescents, vegetables and grains should make up the majority of your diet, followed by seafood/nuts/poultry, fruits and dairy products.

Can I eat junk food?

Junk food, such as chocolates and cold drinks, shouldn't be ingested too often. However, it is okay to have them occasionally. Just remember to eat them in very little amounts.
