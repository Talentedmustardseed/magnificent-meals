How to make chocolate healthy.

  1. Decrease the sugar. Excess sugar can cause tooth decay, and, if you are affected by diabetes or a diabete-related condition, you are at extreme risk of heart disease.

  2. Increase cocoa bean concentration. Cocoa beans are the only healthy thing found in chocolate. They have lots of fibre, and decrease blood pressure, improve cholestrol levels and decrease blood sugar levels.

  3. Remove artificial colours and flavours. Artificial colours and flavours can be found in some chocolates. Some artificial flavours/colours are known to cause cancer.

  4. Replace vanillin with authentic vanilla. Vanillin is a cheap alternative for vvanilla and can be found in some vanilla-flavoured chocolates. Research has shown that this substance can cause allergic reactions, digestive disorders and migraine headaches.

  5. Remove HFCS (High-Fructose corn syrup). High fructose corn syrup is usually used in some chocolate brands in order to make them taste sweeter. HFCS can cause obesity, diabetes, heart disease and cancer.

By removing these substances, chocolate can be way healthier than it used to be. Sure, it may make it taste worse, but it's really a small price to pay considering all the health risks.
